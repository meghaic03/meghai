How it works

01 / Contribute

Small monthly deposits into a pool

Contribute as little as $10/month alongside your existing savings. Funds pool with other participants and invest in low-cost S&P 500 ETFs.

Assets held by Vanguard, Fidelity, US Bank
02 / Grow + Rebalance

Market growth plus a longevity bonus

Your assets grow with the market. When participants exit early, their uncollected returns are redistributed to those who stay — amplifying payouts the longer you live.

Life insurance in reverse — you are rewarded for living longer, not penalized
03 / The Result

Cash payouts at 80, 85, 90, and 95

Starting at age 80, structured cash payouts go directly to you at each milestone. Taxed as long-term capital gains.

Fair Exit Rule — withdraw anytime. Most contributions returned. You are never trapped.
